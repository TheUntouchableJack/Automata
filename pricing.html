Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. You can start with our free Starter plan or begin a 14-day trial of any paid plan without entering a credit card. We believe you should try before you buy.

What happens after my trial ends?

After your 14-day trial, you'll be moved to the free Starter plan. You won't lose any data — just upgrade when you're ready. No surprise charges.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, you'll receive credit toward future billing.

What counts as a "customer"?

A customer is a unique contact in your database. Each email address counts as one customer, regardless of how many projects they're associated with.

What are "AI analyses"?

AI analyses include business diagnostics, automation recommendations, and AI-powered insights. Each time our AI analyzes your business or suggests automations, it uses one credit.

Do unused emails/SMS roll over?

No, monthly allocations reset each billing cycle. However, you can purchase add-on packs at any time if you need more capacity mid-month.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes, we offer 50% off for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact us with proof of nonprofit status to receive your discount.

Need a custom solution?

For large organizations with unique requirements, we offer custom plans with dedicated support, SLAs, and tailored integrations.
